About India

India, the world’s largest democracy, is a land of incredible diversity. With over a billion people, it is home to hundreds of languages, religions, and traditions. India’s history spans thousands of years, from the Indus Valley Civilization to the modern era. It has given the world yoga, Ayurveda, classical music, and breathtaking monuments such as the Taj Mahal.

The nation is also a hub of technology, space exploration, and innovation while remaining deeply rooted in its cultural heritage. From the Himalayas in the north to the Indian Ocean in the south, India’s geography is as varied as its people.
