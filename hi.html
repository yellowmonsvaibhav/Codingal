Amazing Facts About Nikola Tesla

  • He developed the alternating current (AC) electrical system, which is the predominant system used across the world today.
  • Tesla was born during a lightning storm, which the midwife took as a bad omen. His mother replied, "No, he will be a child of light."
  • He had a photographic memory and could visualize inventions in his mind in 3D, enabling him to build and test them mentally.
  • He spoke 8 languages fluently: Serbo-Croatian, Czech, English, French, German, Hungarian, Italian, and Latin.
  • In 1898, he demonstrated the first remote-controlled boat at Madison Square Garden, an invention that was a precursor to modern robotics and drones.
